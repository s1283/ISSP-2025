Quick Album Art Creator
This tool generates simple colored squares for album art placeholders. Click "Generate Album Art" to create images for each genre.
How to use:
1. Click "Generate Album Art"
2. Right-click each image and "Save Image As..."
3. Save to: public/assets/images/
4. Name them: genre-album.jpg (e.g., rock-album.jpg)
